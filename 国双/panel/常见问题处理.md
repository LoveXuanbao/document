### 手动添加panel用户

- 注意修改 b.user_name = 'your_name'

```sql
insert into adminprivilege(privilege_id,permission_id,resource_id,principal_id) select (max(a.privilege_id)+1),1,1,b.principal_id from adminprivilege a, users b where b.user_name='user' group by b.user_id;
```
